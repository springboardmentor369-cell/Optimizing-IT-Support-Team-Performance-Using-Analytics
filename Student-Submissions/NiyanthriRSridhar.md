Quick Overview:
- End-to-End Data Pipeline: Features a complete workflow from raw data cleaning of a customer support ticket dataset to advanced visualization.
- Strategic & Tactical Reporting: Implements a tiered dashboard system that separates executive performance oversight from daily operational backlog management.
- Actionable Business Insights: Utilizes a Category Friction Matrix and Decomposition Tree to identify "Server Down" issues as the primary driver of the 5.30-day average resolution time.
- Geospatial Analysis: Includes interactive map visualizations to pinpoint high-priority ticket concentrations across global regions like the UK and Germany.
- Advanced UX/UI Design: Showcases a custom-built navigation system and consistent visual hierarchy designed for professional, "app-like" user experiences.
